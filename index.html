MedPathway

This is a current personal project which aims to reduce human errors and improve coordination in the medical field. Hospitals can register themselves in the application and organize their patient portals and employee portals. They can also assign patients to specific employees and make timelines and checklists for each patient. There is also a feature where if the patient needs surgery there is a surgery assistant provided along with a voice assistant. Patients are also able to login and access their portals.
